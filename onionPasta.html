Chicken Lo Mien

Onion Pasta - Delicious and easy to make succulent Onion Pasta, great for the whole family!

  • Prep Time: 5mins
  • Cook Time: 15mins
  • Noodles Cooking Time: 10mins
  • Total Time: 30mins

INGREDIENTS

  • Â½ cup olive oil
  • Water, for boiling noodles
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 5 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon chicken bouillon
  • 1 pound uncooked pasta, cooked according to package directions

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a big pot boil enough water to boil. When the water comes to a roaring boil, add noodles to it and cook till al-dente.
  2. Once done, add drain noodles and add 1tsp of oil, mix lightly and nicely (can use hands) and spread on a flat plate/dish.
  3. In a large skillet place oil, butter and onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add basil, pepper, water and bouillon and cook on low heat for 10 minutes.
  5. Add onion mixture to cooked pasta, sprinkle with cheese, toss and serve.
